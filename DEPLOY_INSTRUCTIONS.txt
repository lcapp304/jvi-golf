# JVI Golf — Deployment Instructions
# How to get this on everyone's phone in ~5 minutes

## What you'll end up with
A link like https://jvi-golf.vercel.app that anyone can open and add to
their home screen like a real app — no App Store required.

---

## STEP 1 — Install Node.js (one-time, 2 minutes)
If you've never done this before:
1. Go to https://nodejs.org
2. Click the big green "LTS" download button
3. Run the installer — just click Next through everything
4. Restart your computer when it's done

---

## STEP 2 — Create a free GitHub account (one-time)
1. Go to https://github.com
2. Click "Sign up" — use any email
3. Verify your email address

---

## STEP 3 — Upload your project to GitHub
1. Go to https://github.com/new
2. Repository name: jvi-golf
3. Keep it Public (required for free Vercel)
4. Click "Create repository"
5. On the next page, click "uploading an existing file"
6. Drag and drop the entire "jvi-golf" folder's CONTENTS
   (package.json, vite.config.js, index.html, src folder, public folder)
7. Click "Commit changes"

---

## STEP 4 — Deploy on Vercel (2 minutes)
1. Go to https://vercel.com
2. Click "Sign Up" → choose "Continue with GitHub"
3. Authorize Vercel to access your GitHub
4. Click "Add New Project"
5. Find "jvi-golf" in the list → click "Import"
6. Vercel auto-detects it as a Vite project — don't change anything
7. Click "Deploy"
8. Wait about 60 seconds...
9. You'll see "Congratulations!" and your live URL

---

## STEP 5 — Share the link
Send the URL (e.g. https://jvi-golf.vercel.app) to everyone in your group.

---

## How players install it on their phone

### iPhone (Safari only — must use Safari, not Chrome):
1. Open the link in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow pointing up)
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"
5. The JVI Golf icon appears on their home screen

### Android (Chrome):
1. Open the link in Chrome
2. Tap the three-dot menu (top right)
3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
4. Tap "Add"
5. The JVI Golf icon appears on their home screen

---

## Updating the app later
If you ever want to make changes (new teams, tweak anything):
1. Make the change in Claude
2. Download the new App.jsx file
3. Go to your GitHub repo → src/App.jsx → click the pencil icon
4. Paste the new code → click "Commit changes"
5. Vercel automatically redeploys in about 60 seconds
6. Everyone gets the update next time they open the app

---

## Your data is safe
All scores and teams are saved in localStorage on each device.
The admin enters teams before the outing — those stay saved until Reset is hit.

---

## Need a custom URL?
Vercel lets you set a custom domain for free if you own one,
or you can pay ~$10/year for a domain like jvigolf.com at Namecheap or GoDaddy
and connect it to Vercel in about 5 minutes.
